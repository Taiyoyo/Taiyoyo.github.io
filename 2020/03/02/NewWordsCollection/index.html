第一讲：读数字，懂单词

发表于 2020-03-02

新词新义

Words Pronunciation Meaning Auxiliary
atone /əˈtəʊn/ to act in a way to show sorry for doing something wrong in the past 亚当和夏娃想和上帝重归于好
auxiliary /ɔːɡˈzɪliəri/ giving help or support to somebody or something
intersection /ˈɪntərsekʃn/ a place where two or more roads, lines, etc. meet or cross each other 词源自inter和section，处于各个部分相交之处，交叉路口
pentagon /ˈpentəɡɑːn/ a flat shape with five straight sides and five angles penta- 通 five，五边形，Pentagon专有名词五角大楼
quarantine /ˈkwɔːrəntiːn/ a period of time when an animal or a person that has or may have a disease is kept away from others in order to prevent the disease from spreading 意大利大规模黑死病蔓延时，进威尼斯水城的船要再港口外隔离四十天才能进城
salvage /ˈsælvɪdʒ/ 抢救；挽救 词源自save，自带拯救的含义
solicit /səˈlɪsɪt/ 卖淫；招募；征集 词源自one和cite，把人喊过来成为自己的一部分
transitive /ˈtrænzətɪv/ used with a direct object 出现在词典释义中，带有及物性质的
twilight /ˈtwaɪlaɪt/ the small amount of light or the period of time at the end of a day after the sun has gone down 词源自two和light，twi- 表示二分之一，内涵衰落
unicorn /ˈjuːnɪkɔːrn/ an animal like a white horse with a long straight horn on his head 词源自horn，打仗吹的号角，音变成corn，派生词为corner，角落

旧词新解

Known Words Auxiliary
ounce 一盎司约等于500克，黄金的计量单位为盎司，源自熔融状态下的液体黄金
sin 将si- 颠倒为 is，be动词表示存在，
uniform uni- 表示统一，统一的形式即制服
unite 元音u通o，i通e，其实unite 源自one，变成一体，表示联盟、联合
